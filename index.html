About Us

SwarAum is a contemporary music band that blends traditional sounds with modern beats, creating a unique musical experience. Formed in 2018, our journey has been about exploring the depths of music and connecting with audiences worldwide.

Our music is a fusion of various genres, creating a sound that's both familiar and innovative. We believe in the power of music to transcend boundaries and bring people together.
